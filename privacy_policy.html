Privacy Policy

Instagram Photos

We use Elfsight Instagram feed plugin to display photo widgets. This fetches information about photos from particular username or hashtag. We use only basic data like image links, tags and captions. We do not store Instagram images and videos in our storage in any way. Images on our website comes directly from Instagram.

Email

When subscribing to our newsletter, we save your email address. If at any time you wish for us to copy, modify or remove your information, you may do so by sending us an email through support@cityscape360.com. You can unsubscribe at any time and your information will be removed.

Commenting

We use Disqus comment plugin to display and store user comments. Disqus has data protection regulations in place to protect user information. Signing into Disqus, Facebook, Twitter, or Google+, to post a comment means increased validation. Disqus has their own privacy policy with detailed information.
