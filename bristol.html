Food & Drink

Bristol is best known for producing great tasting cider, and you'll find a range of independent cafés and restaurants to choose from. The city has also been named as the vegan capital of the world in 2019. Here are some of the top student-friendly eateries in Bristol:

  • – St Nick's Market
  • – ZaZa Bazaar (world buffet)
  • – Swoon (ice-cream!)
  • – Pinkmans Bakery
  • – Boston Tea Party
  • – Bravas (Spanish tapas)
  • – The Bristolian Café
  • – Thali Café (Indian cuisine)

Check out this student blog for more suggestions on where to eat in Bristol.

Popular Events & Festivals

  • March: Bristol Comic Con & Gaming Festival
  • April: Funderworld Theme Park
  • May: Foodies Festival, Love Saves The Day music festival
  • June: DogFest, Bristol Sounds music festival
  • July: Harbour Festival, Bristol Pride, Bristol Comedy Garden
  • August: International Balloon Fiesta, The Downs Music Festival
  • September: Bristol Open Doors, South Glos Food & Drink Festival
  • October: Wild & Well Festival
  • November: The Festival of Light at Longleat
  • December: Christmas market
