Inspirational travel blogger of the month...

Both have built up a massive following on Instagram due to their incredible photography skills showcasing their travel adventures. Originating from the UK, Jack quit his full-time job to follow his dream of going travelling in which he is now getting paid to share and promote destinations. He also blogs about how he takes and edits his photos to get them to look so good.
